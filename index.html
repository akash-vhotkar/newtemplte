Website Development

Also known as web programming. It involves creation and development of awebsite and hosting the same via internet or intranet. It is customized as per the client’s needs and requirements. It ranges from coding to non-design aspects, from writing markups to complete programming of the website.

GRAPHIC DESIGN

It is an art with a goal. It includes creating designs for the website using images, symbols, logos and much more. It is an expression of ideas using various elements and tools thereby connecting with the target audience.

BRAND IDENTITY

Brand Management is a broad term that includes placement, marketing, advertising, design and distribution that augments and develops brand personality. Effective Brand Management helps in building a loyal customer base and enables high levels of retention. A brand may include an individual person, organization, service product or an event.

DIGITAL MARKETING

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) and Social Media Optimization (SMO) have become an integral part of any business. SEO and SMO are done to optimize the ranking of a website on search engines and different social platforms. The purpose is to increase the visitors on the website therefore getting maximum business out of it.
